About Follow up

Camera's have been incorporated into the daily lives of nearly everyone! We now view our cell phones as the center of how we perform nearly every action of our lives. One of the greatest aspects of our mobile devices are the cameras located at the front and back. This camera however, can only record a small area infront of it. Follow up allows its users to circumvent this issue and follows you wherever you go!
